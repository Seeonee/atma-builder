DEFAULT MOVES Rumble When you fight a nearby foe, roll+tough or +hasty. On a 7+, harm or otherwise impact them. Barrage When you shoot a distant foe, roll+calm or +sly. On a 7+, harm or otherwise displace them. Realize When you observe something, roll+bright or +calm. On a 7+, the GM will tell a secret about it, and ask how you knew/noticed. Coerce When you offer someone a deal, roll+sly or +tough. On a 7+, they accept after setting terms. Survive When you defy danger, roll+hasty or +bright. On a 7+, reduce or redirect the GM's move.
